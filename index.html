ATLAS Furniture

About us

Located in the Sunset District of San Francisco, Altas provides its customers with elegant furnishings and décor to embellish their home. While offering a sizeable array of pieces for customers to choose from, they also make custom designs for customers looking to achieve a particular look or blending of styles.

Our Mission

PRICE. We strive to offer the best prices especially given the sleek and luxurious looks of our products. We dont cut corners, and we avoid any unessesary price inflations.

QUALITY. Our products are made from materials that are both durable and refined. We drive to source all our materials from either renewable or fair-trade sources, and commit a portion of every purchase towards global ecology.

CUSTOMER. Lastly, we aim to create the best experience for our customers. Whether it be custom work or delivering on great products. We work with our customer on every step of the process to ensure we achieve exaclty what they want.
